About

I'm Javier Rodríguez, a 21-year-old Computer Science student from Valencia (Spain).
I'm currently studing a Master's degree of Distributed computing at UPV, and plan on continuing my education after that, probably with a PhD.
At the moment I'm working at ITI as a researcher, on the Distributed Systems group.
